Frequently asked questions

Answers to most common questions.

Q. What are credits?

A. Credits are our form of currency. These will be what you use to buy things like extra food, vending machine snacks, drinks, etc.

Q. Do you have any hidden fees?

A. No we do not. The contract presents all of our terms and conditions, as well as any fees and taxes. Although, make sure you read the fine print.

Q. What payment methods do you accept?

A. We accept any payment methods, cash, credit, debit, etc. All we ask is a 20% deposit on your class selection, and you will not be refunded this if you cancel a month or sooner to the departure.

Q. Is it safe?

A. Yes, the experience and equipment is very safe. We have highly trained professionals that do daily examinations on all of our products/equipment to make sure they function properly. We also run weekly tests. We cover any costs of injuries or anything that may occur while on the trip.

Q. What is your refund policy?

A.We will refund you 100% of your money (excluding your deposit) only if you cancel your trip a month in advance. If you cancel closer to the launch than a month, then you can only receive a refund of half of your original payment, but the deposit will not be refunded.

Q. How can I contact you?

A. You can check our contact page for an email, phone number, and more. Contact us if you experience any problems or have any questions.
